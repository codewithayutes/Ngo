About Us

Welcome to Small Efforts Big Smile, a community-driven NGO dedicated to spreading kindness through small yet meaningful actions. What started as a simple initiative—feeding children, animals, and those in need—has grown into a movement of compassion and social change. Through our efforts, we aim to ensure that no one goes hungry and that love and care reach those who need it the most. We believe that even the smallest act of kindness can bring a big smile, and together, we can create a world where generosity and empathy thrive. From distributing meals to underprivileged communities to rescuing and feeding stray animals, we document our journey on social media to inspire others to join us. Now, with our website, we’re expanding our mission—connecting with more people, raising awareness, and making it easier for you to contribute. Join us in making a difference, one small effort at a time. Because a single act of kindness can create ripples of happiness!
